Insight

From the minute you step into the College of Veterinary Medicine you'll be part of a team of forward-looking healthcare professionals who are committed to the health and wellness of animals.

At its heart is our world-class, Purdue Veterinary Teaching Hospital filled with committed faculty who teach real-world skills and a problem-solving mindset - graduating “practice-ready” veterinarians and veterinary technicians (the veterinary medical nursing profession).
